The Kobaya Experience

Creative. Modern. Elevated.

Kobaya Catering is South Florida's premier partner in luxury sushi catering. We bring outside-of-the-box sushi fusion, new flavors, and meticulous attention to detail to every experience we create.

Frequently Asked Questions

よくある質問

What areas do you serve?

+

We serve all of South Florida, including Miami, Fort Lauderdale, Palm Beach, Boca Raton, and surrounding areas. For events outside this region, please contact us to discuss arrangements.

What types of events do you cater?

+

We cater private dinner parties, corporate galas, weddings and celebrations, VIP and celebrity events, venue partnerships, and intimate omakase experiences. Any size group is welcome.

Can the menu be customized?

+

Absolutely. Every Kobaya menu is fully customizable. Whether your guests have allergies, dietary preferences, or specific requests, we craft the experience accordingly.

Do you offer sake and wine pairings?

+

Yes! We offer sake and wine pairing options curated by our Advanced Sommelier. We can also host dedicated wine and sake tasting classes.

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance. For peak seasons — especially around Miami F1 Grand Prix, Art Basel, and holidays — please reach out as early as possible.

Do you provide all equipment and setup?

+

Yes. We arrive with everything needed — from our sushi station and premium ingredients to plating, presentation, and cleanup. You simply enjoy.
